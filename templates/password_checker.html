💪 Length Matters

Each additional character exponentially increases the time needed to crack it. 12+ characters is the minimum for strong passwords.

🔀 Mix Characters

Combine uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. A mix of all four makes dictionary attacks nearly impossible.

"Password123!" looks complex but is one of the first guesses in any dictionary attack. Avoid predictable substitutions.
